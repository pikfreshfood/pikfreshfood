How to Make Nigerian Jollof Rice

📍 Showing vendors near: Yaba, Lagos
📋 Ingredients You'll Need
  • 🍚 2 cups Long-grain Rice + Add
  • 🍅 4 Large Tomatoes + Add
  • 🧅 2 Onions + Add
  • 🥦 1 Red Bell Pepper + Add
  • 🌶️ Scotch Bonnet Pepper + Add
  • 🧈 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil + Add
  • 🧂 Salt & Seasoning to taste + Add
🍚

Jollof Rice is one of West Africa's most beloved dishes. This Nigerian version features perfectly seasoned rice cooked in a rich tomato base until each grain is infused with flavour. Here's how to make authentic Nigerian Jollof Rice that will impress your family!

👨‍🍳 Cooking Steps
Wash rice 2-3 times under running water until water runs clear. Set aside to drain.
Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions together (save some onions sliced). Sieve for smooth sauce.
Heat oil in pot and fry onions until golden brown. This adds depth to the rice.
Add tomato blended mixture to the pot and cook for 5-8 mins until it reduces by half.
Add stock cubes, salt, and seasoning. Taste and adjust as needed.
Add washed rice, coat with sauce, and stir. Add 4 cups of stock or water.
Bring to boil, then reduce heat low. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes until rice is tender.
Fluff with fork and let rest for 2 minutes. Your Jollof Rice is ready!
